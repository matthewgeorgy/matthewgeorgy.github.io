Types

Below is a chart illustrating the type matchups and effectiveness.
The vertical type is the attacking type, the horizontal type is the defending type.

Type Chart

Although not shown in this chart, type matchups also multiply together:

For example, Rock is 2x effective against Ice or Flying. Thus, it would be 4x effective against a Pokemon that is both Ice and Flying Type.

Furthermore, Grass is ½x effective against Fire or Dragon Thus, it would be ¼× effective against a Pokemon that is both Fire and Dragon Type.

Finally, Grass is 2x effective against Water and ½x effective against Flying. Thus, it would be 1x effective (or normally effective) against a Pokemon that is both Water and Flying Type.
