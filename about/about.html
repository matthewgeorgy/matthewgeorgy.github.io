The Project

I originally started this project as a way to practice and improve my skills in HTML & CSS.
I had an idea in mind for how I wanted this site to look - and I stuck to it for the most part -
but of course made some changes along the way.

This was mainly made possible thanks to my background in design.
I took Technological Design courses in Grades 11 & 12 in High School - where I learned the principles
of design and methodologies behind it - as well as being a member of my High School's FIRST Robotics Team -
where I was a part of the design team (the members responsible for designing the robot).

As time went on, and this project started gaining more complexity, I recognized its potential as a project for my resume.
Thus, I decided to use this project as a way of showcasing BOTH my HTML / CSS skills, AND my design capabilities.

The main source for this project was Bulbapedia, which is one of the largest - perhaps even the largest -
Pokemon encyclopedias on the Internet .
As stated on the home page, the information on this site is entirely specific to Generation 5.
Bulbapedia is an amazing source for information on all things related to Pokemon, so I highly suggest that
you go there if you wish to learn more!
